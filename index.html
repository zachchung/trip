⛰️ Mountain Passes
2,239m
Pordoi Pass (Passo Pordoi)
The highest pass on the Great Dolomite Road. Cable car ascends to Sass Pordoi (2,950m) for breathtaking 360° views.
Day 2 — Great Dolomite Rd
2,105m
Falzarego Pass (Passo Falzarego)
Gateway between Cortina d'Ampezzo and the western Dolomites. Part of the Great Dolomite Road. WWI history with Lagazuoi tunnels nearby.
Day 2 — Great Dolomite Rd
2,121m
Gardena Pass (Passo Gardena)
Links Corvara in Badia with Val Gardena. Part of the Sellaronda ski circuit. Dramatic views of the Puez-Odle Nature Park.
Day 5 Route
2,218m
Sella Pass (Passo Sella)
One of the four legendary Sellaronda passes. Connects Val Gardena with Val di Fassa. Surrounded by the imposing Sella Massif.
